# Marketing Content Strategy - JobAI South Africa

## Target Audience
- Unemployed South Africans (35%)
- Job seekers wanting career change (25%)
- Graduates entering job market (20%)
- Professionals seeking better opportunities (15%)
- Foreign nationals with work permits (5%)

## Primary Platforms & Content Types

### 1. TikTok (@JobAI_SA)
**Content Strategy:**
- Daily success stories (15-30 second clips)
- AI CV transformation comparisons
- Job application tips specific to SA market
- Behind-the-scenes AI application process
- Testimonials from employed applicants
- Industry-specific job hunting tips

**Hashtags:**
- #JobAISA #FindJobsSA #AICV #SAGraduate
- #JobSearchSA #CareerHelpSA #GetHiredSA
- #CapeTownJobs #JoburgJobs #RemoteWorkSA

### 2. Facebook Page & Groups
**Content Strategy:**
- Longer success stories (2-3 minute videos)
- Live Q&A sessions with career coaches
- Industry-specific job market updates
- CV workshop webinars
- Company partnerships announcements
- Daily job match highlights

**Groups to Target:**
- South African Jobs
- Cape Town Jobs
- Johannesburg Jobs
- Graduate Jobs South Africa
- Remote Work South Africa

### 3. Instagram (@JobAI_SA)
**Content Strategy:**
- Visual CV transformations (before/after)
- Infographics: Job market statistics
- Reels: Quick job application tips
- Stories: Daily application updates
- IGTV: Success interviews
- Carousels: Step-by-step guides

### 4. YouTube Channel
**Content Strategy:**
- Detailed success case studies
- How-to guides for using platform
- Industry expert interviews
- AI technology explainers
- Monthly progress reports
- Virtual job fair coverage

### 5. LinkedIn Company Page
**Content Strategy:**
- Professional success stories
- Industry insights and reports
- Partnership announcements
- Employee testimonials
- Corporate responsibility initiatives
- B2B partnerships

- Clear pricing disclosure
- Data privacy emphasis

- Transparent AI capabilities
- No guaranteed employment promises
